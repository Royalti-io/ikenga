C · /finance/inter-company · reconciliation

True N×N matrix chart — rows are the entity that owes, columns are the entity that is owed by. Off-diagonal cells show the directional net balance (USD-normalized): green = row is owed, red = row owes. Diagonal cells are self-pairs (—). Heat bar on the left edge of each cell scales to magnitude so you can scan the chart for outliers without reading numbers. Click any cell to open the entry list (sheet pattern). The reconciliation queue lives below the chart.

F · single-view panes · standalone routes

Sections A–E presented Finance as a tabbed mini-system inside the Accounting wrapper. But in real workspace usage, a user often pins /finance/transactions (or any other sub-route) as its own pane — the Accounting header and sub-tab strip disappear, the pane chrome is just the content-tabs bar from shell.css, and the route stands as a single leaf in a multi-pane layout. These thumbnails demonstrate that shape — same chrome contract as 02-pane-anatomy and 03-pane-route, scaled down to "this is one of three panes in your workspace" size.
